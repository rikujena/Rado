Rado is a ride-sharing platform that lets you post or find rides to your destination. Whether you're a driver with empty seats or someone looking for a cost-effective travel option, Rado helps you share the ride, split the cost, and reduce fuel usage. Fewer vehicles mean less CO₂ and a cleaner, greener planet.
